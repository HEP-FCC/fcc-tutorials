Learning Objectives

  • Where to ask questions

Eventually you will get stuck when trying to do something. This lesson is about how to get help with getting unstuck.

Mailing lists

For FCC specific questions your best bet are the FCC mailing list : FCC-PED-SoftwareAndComputing-Documentation@cern.ch A recent alternative to the mailing lists is the Mattermost chat having dedicated channels, and the discourse forum

For more general questions Stack Overflow and Google are good starting places.

FCC glossary

A broad range of FCC-related terms, abbreviations and definitions can be found in the FCC Glossary linked from the main experiment page. Feel free to contribute.
